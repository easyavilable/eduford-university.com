Undergraduate

An undergraduate degree (also called first degree or simply degree) is a colloquial term for an academic degree earned by a person who has completed undergraduate courses. it is usually offered at an institution of higher education, such as a college or university.

Graduate

Graduate education encompasses research, study and teaching beyond the bachelor's degree. While undergraduate education leads to a bachelor's degree, graduate education leads to master's degree and doctorate, so we can also called a doctoral degree.
